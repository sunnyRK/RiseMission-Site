Fair by design

It's your alarm, not your warden.

A hard alarm shouldn't punish you for a dark room or a bad camera angle. Rise Mission is built to be tough and forgiving.

  • Every physical challenge has a mental backup. Camera can't see you, room's too dark, or you can't safely do push-ups? The backup runs instead — and your streak stays safe.
  • Emergency dismiss is always there. Hold five seconds, confirm, and the alarm stops. It breaks your perfect streak, but you're never trapped.
  • Honest about the limits. The system Stop button always exists — we record wake-ups truthfully instead of pretending an alarm is impossible to turn off.
Private by default

Your mornings never leave your phone.

No account. No ads. No tracking. No servers of ours that your data could travel to.

  • Camera stays on-device. Push-up and QR frames are analyzed in memory with Apple's Vision framework and immediately discarded — never recorded, saved, or uploaded.
  • Nothing to collect. Alarms, streaks and history live only on your iPhone. The App Store privacy label says it plainly: Data Not Collected.
  • No strings. Free in 1.0, no in-app purchases, no sign-in. Delete the app and the data goes with it.
